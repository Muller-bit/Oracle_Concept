### ðŸ”— Chainlink Data Feeds Fundamentals

Chainlink Data Feeds allow you to fetch real-world data such as asset prices, reserve balances, and L2 sequencer health and use that data in your smart contract.

- Data feeds provide many different types of data for your applications:

- Price Feeds: aggregated source crypto-asset prices.

- SmartData Feeds: a suite of onchain data offerings designed to unlock the utility, accessibility, and reliability of tokenized real-world assets (RWAs)

- Rate and Volatility Feeds: data for interest rates, interest rate curves, and asset volatility.

- L2 Sequencer Uptime Feeds: Identify if an L2 sequencer is available.
